Dieting tips

  • Avoid processed foods
  • Prioritize non-processed and nutrient dense food
  • Gaining muscle mass
    • High calorie intake - eating in a caloric surplus (in order to build muscle mass, this is a must) - EAT HARD
    • Proper-form and volume training - TRAIN HARD
    • High protein intake - eat 2g of proteins for kg of bodyweight (2g+ per lb)
    • Proper fat intake/don't eliminate all fats from your diet, as they're fundamental for building sex hormones

      • This is particularly important to note. Fats, and especially Cholesterol, are very important to your hormonal health, since they consist in the building blocks for the creation of sex hormones (testosterone, ...).

  • Losing fat mass
    • Limit excessive fat intake
    • Avoid sugary foods

      Sugar is useless for you apart from when it comes from fruit and other health and natural sources, such as honey.

Excercise tips



  • Maximise training to failure
  • Train to 3 to 5 times a week - individual variances matter significantly
  • Good form is everything - don't cheat and don't ego-lift (for this i suggest to follow some YouTube channels)
  • Don't underlook cardio - this is important
